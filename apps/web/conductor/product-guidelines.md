# Product Guidelines

## Target Audience

The primary target users for "Treno Chat Local" are persons that are physically present in the same space/venue. This could be in a train, on a beach, in a pub/disco, in a hotel, or at an event.

## Tone and Style

The primary tone and style of communication is casual and friendly. It should facilitate informal, peer-to-peer conversations among users, promoting a relaxed and engaging atmosphere.

## Visual Identity

The visual identity should be functional and intuitive. Emphasis is on clear navigation, readable fonts, and accessible design to support diverse user needs and quick information retrieval.

## Brand Messaging

The brand messaging should focus on Community and Connection. We emphasize fostering interaction and shared experiences among individuals in close proximity.
